#### Thesis Research — University of Bologna, Italy
*Jan 2025 – Present · Remote / Bologna*

Independently secured an international research collaboration at the Department of Physics and Astronomy (DiFA), University of Bologna, under **Prof. Federico Marulli**. Working on the **Back-in-Time Void Finder (BitVF)** within CosmoBolognaLib — an open-source cosmology library — implementing and validating void-finding algorithms against observational and simulated data.

**Key contributions:**
- Designed and executed a reproducible analysis pipeline for large-scale structure data
- Benchmarked dynamical void-finder against REVOLVER/ZOBOV geometric catalogs
- Validated robustness under severe tracer down-sampling (MultiDark-Patchy mock datasets)
- Structured uncertainty quantification framework for cosmological inference
